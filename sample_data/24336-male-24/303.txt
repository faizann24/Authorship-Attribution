Texas toast does not taste like ass.  Here is a quick recipe for cheesy bread I just whipped up:  Cheesy Texas Bread Preparation time: 5 minutes Cooking time: 8 minutes  Ingredients: Texas toast, sliced thick (3 slices) Shredded mozzarella or sharp cheddar cheese (1 cup) Grated parmesan cheese (3 tablespoons) Butter Garlic powder Italian seasoning Red pepper flakes  Directions: 1)  Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 2)  Lightly butter the bread. 3)  Sprinkle a tablespoon of parmesan onto the buttered side of each slice. 4)  Season with garlic powder, italian seasoning and red pepper flakes to taste. 5)  Cover each slice with 1/3rd cup of shredded cheese. 6)  Place onto an ungreased cookie sheet. 7)  Cook for 8 minutes.
